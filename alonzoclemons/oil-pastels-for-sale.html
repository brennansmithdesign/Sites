Oil Pastels for Sale

In the summertime, when the sculpting materials begin to feel "tacky" from the warmer temperatures, Alonzo turns to his other form of artistic expression, his bold and distinctive oil pastels.  In making the drawings, Alonzo does not so much pull from his "savant wiring" as from his ongoing urge to create; therefore, the pastels have a very different quality than his sculptures.

By making these, he also creates an art form that is accessible for purchase by more of his supporters, selling them at $100 (plus shipping/handling).  Most of them are 24" x 36," and sold in simple poster frames.  Contact us at Gifted Hands if interested.
